    Melvin Alvarez is
a Web, Visual, and
New Media Designer.

You can follow me on
twitter, instagram,
github, or VSCO.

Or send me an email.

I am interested in all forms of Visual Communication, particularly tools and techniques that bridge the gap between people and the promise of something good.

For me, design means understanding. The act of listening intently allows the discovery of something more than justsurface level. As a designer, I think part of our job is to look beyond what we see to be able to truly understand what's right in front us, filter out the noise, and synthesize a solution formulated from a process that is heavily anchored to functionality while also tapping into beauty and its immense power to inspire.

I was born in Maasin, Southern Leyte in the Philippines. I grew up playing video games, reading books, and running to the beach to swim whenever I can.
