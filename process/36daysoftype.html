The Flood Preparedness Kit was designed to inform flood-prone communities about flooding. Core77

A Flood Preparedness Kit made out of strips of ductape on a 6 x 8 foot tarpaulin. It provides four multi-functional transformations: 1 - A calendar providing information on average monthly water levels and temperatures. 2 - An information board providing information on storm signals, wind strength, emergency hotlines, and proper safety measures. 3 - A supplementary emergency toolkit that can hold various important tools and items for flood safety and survival. 4 - And a 6 x 8 foot SOS Distress Signal that can aid in search and rescue as well as protection against harmful elements. It is designed to be placed in public schools in the Philippines and other designated evacuation shelters in respective barangays (communities). It also aims to educate the community about flooding, the factors that affect it and proper precautionary measures to ensure safety.

The initial process mainly involved visualizing complex information on elements that affect flooding intensities. One of my main goals was to be able to find a way to be able to display the information with the restricted space and tools.

Materiality heavily influenced the overall approach to both visualizing data and designing interactions and the transformations that each interaction would provide. Print signatures were one of the main inspirations for the project, considering how each fold reveals new information. With that idea in mind, I thought about the certain stages of flood preparedness including during the occurence of flooding as well as past it.

The first transformation is a 24 x 36 inch poster/calendar that visualizes monthly average water levels.

The second transformation is a 48 x 36 inch information board that provides helpful emergency contact information as well as a more comprehensive guide in the form of an insert. It is designed to cater to people in the community so translations are made for the users to easily understand the information thats been provided.
